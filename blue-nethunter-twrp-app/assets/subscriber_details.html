Subscription Details

Subscribers are able to download TWRP recovery images directly within the app instead of downloading through a web browser. Once the file is downloaded, the TWRP app automatically checks the hash of the downloaded file to ensure that the file was downloaded correctly and completely. If you have enabled root permissions, you will be able to flash the downloaded recovery image without having to browse to and select the file on supported devices. Additional subscriber benefits may be added in the future.

Ways To Become A Subscriber

There are two ways to become a subscriber. You may enable the network statistics feature of the app which involves a small amount of anonymous data collection related to network performance. The network statistics subscriber method is free and never expires (see terms and conditions for more details). Alternatively, you may subscribe via Google Play for 1 year for a small donation to help offset our costs of hosting the recovery images.

NOTE: Do not buy a subscription if your device is not currently supported! Purchasing a subscription is not a way to purchase support for your device!

We may have to end support for any device at any time. Your subscription is a donation for our past efforts, not a payment for future support. Subscribers do not receive any additional support for problems you may encounter when using TWRP.
